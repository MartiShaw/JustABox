Different types of pollution.

There are five main types of pollution: air pollution, land pollution, light pollution, water pollution, and noise pollution.

Air pollution is releasing poisonous gases into the air, which can cause the ozone layer to disappear if we do not do something about it. This is important because if the ozone layer disappears, dangerous solar ultraviolet (UV) rays to enter the atmostphere. This will greately increase the chance of getting skin cancer, and cause the skin to burn from the rays.

    Light Pollution

Light pollution may not affect humans, but it affects nature. Light pollution is caused by placing many street lamps without the cone-shape, causing the natural hours to become disoriented. Some effects of light pollution include birds singing at unusual hours, plant's natural develpoement to become off-track, sea turtles -who use the starlight to go into the ocean-to go the wrong way, towards the sreetlights.

    Noise Pollution

Noise pollution affects everyone, despite not being able to see or smell the pollution. Noise pollution happens when sounds made by industrial factories or planes reaches a very harmful sound. This can lead to hearing loss, stress-related illness, speech interference, and high blood pressure.

    Water Pollution

Water pollution, like land pollution, causes parts of the water to become unusuable/undrinkable due to the water being poisonous to health. This can become a very serious problem soon if we do not fix it now because the world would become void of drinkable water.
